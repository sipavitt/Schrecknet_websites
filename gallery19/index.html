Gallery19

About

Hidden in the vaulted arches of Camden Stables Market, Gallery19 curates a rare collection of contemporary works that blur the line between desire and dread. Our focus is on immersive, emotionally provocative pieces—works that breathe, stalk, whisper, and haunt long after the viewer steps away.

Founded by the elusive artist Nola McKellan, Gallery19 maintains her signature contradiction: sumptuous darkness threaded with a quiet, unsettling intimacy. McKellan’s refusal to appear publicly has only intensified the mystique surrounding both her work and the artists she selects.

Current Exhibition

“Anatomies of Light” explores the devouring relationship between beauty and shadow—featuring new works from resident and guest artists personally selected by McKellan. Private viewings available by appointment.

Featured Artists

Our represented artists explore attraction, violence, surrealism, and the subconscious. Each body of work is chosen for its ability to resonate at a level beyond comfort.
