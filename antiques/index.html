A Boutique of the Unusual

Nestled within the narrow lanes of Seven Dials, Pearse & Penumbra has traded in antiques, ephemera, and natural wonders for more than a century. We specialise in the curious and the quietly extraordinary — objects that whisper stories, and artefacts whose provenance is as fascinating as their form.
