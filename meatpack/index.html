About Us

Based in the historic Billingsgate district of London, Billingsgate Meat Packing Co. has supplied high‑quality, professionally prepared meat products to traders, restaurants, and wholesalers for over four decades. We pride ourselves on traditional workmanship, consistency, and a business‑first approach built on reliability.

  • Cold‑chain delivery across Greater London
  • Specialist trimming and preparation
