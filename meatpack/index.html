About Us

Operating from Spitalfields for over forty years, Spitalfields Meat Packing Co. supplies restaurants, caterers, and private clients with reliably prepared meat products. Our reputation is built on consistency, trust, and the careful handling of sensitive stock.

We are accustomed to meeting unusual requirements and operating outside standard hours when necessary.

Specialist & Exotic Meats

In addition to conventional red meats, we routinely process a limited range of specialist and exotic stock for approved clients.

  • Ostrich
  • Kangaroo
  • Wild boar
  • Special order consignments
  • Other non-standard preparations by arrangement

Availability varies and enquiries are assessed on a case-by-case basis.

Sourcing & Provenance

We work with a discreet network of suppliers across Greater London and the South East, allowing us to respond quickly to time-sensitive consignments.

Our sourcing model prioritises traceability, controlled handover, and minimal transit time. Certain materials are received via institutional partners and intermediary facilities where specialist handling is required.

Full provenance documentation is maintained internally and made available to regulators upon request.

Our Facility

Our Spitalfields facility combines traditional cutting rooms with modern cold storage. Segregated preparation areas allow for specialist handling and ensure that every consignment is processed appropriately.

Discrete access points ensure uninterrupted operations.

Product Range

We offer a broad selection of red meats and specialist cuts, prepared to specification. Long-standing clients value our willingness to source less common stock and fulfil exacting requests.
