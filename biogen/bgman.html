Meet Our Leadership Team

BioGen Industries is guided by experienced scientific and business leaders who are committed to advancing haemological research and ensuring responsible, impactful innovation across the organisation.

Dr Octavian Juul

Managing Director

Dr Juul began his scientific career in the early 1970s, completing a doctorate in neurochemistry at the Université Descartes in Paris. His early research focused on metabolic deficiencies and enzyme-linked blood disorders, work that contributed to the development of several modern treatment approaches.

After serving as a senior researcher at the Wellside Foundation, Dr Juul co-founded BioGen Industries with Dr Katerina Marin. Under his leadership, the organisation has expanded its clinical partnerships, supported numerous early-career researchers, and established a stable platform for long-term haemological research.

Although he stepped back from active laboratory work in 2007 to focus on strategic development, Dr Juul remains deeply involved in BioGen’s future direction and maintains oversight of research governance across the company.

Dr Katerina Marin

Director of Research

Dr Marin began her career as an endocrinologist at East Tallinn Central Hospital, later completing a research doctorate focused on clotting disorders, particularly Plasminogen Activator Inhibitor Type 1 deficiency. Her work spans multiple international research institutions, including the Mayo Clinic in Los Angeles and the National Research Centre for Haematology in Moscow.

As Director of Research, Dr Marin oversees BioGen’s scientific portfolio and leads initiatives ranging from rare disease investigation to behavioural and biochemical studies associated with bloodborne illnesses. She has published extensively on the interplay between blood composition, addiction pathways, and long-term physiological outcomes.

Dr Marin continues to supervise several active projects and remains a key contributor to BioGen’s research strategy and academic collaborations.

Johannes Rehema

Head of People

With an extensive background in organisational strategy and people management, Mr Rehema serves as BioGen’s senior business manager and oversees internal operations, staff development, and corporate governance. He completed his MBA at the University of Cambridge Judge Business School, graduating with highest honours.

Mr Rehema’s early career included consultancy roles at PricewaterhouseCoopers and Deloitte, where he specialised in restructuring, organisational culture, and large-scale operational improvement. His transition to BioGen brought a renewed focus on aligning research growth with high standards of staff wellbeing, regulatory compliance, and long-term strategic planning.

He continues to guide BioGen’s organisational development and oversees recruitment, HR policy, and internal support frameworks across the company.
