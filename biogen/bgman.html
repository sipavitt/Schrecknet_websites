Meet Our Leadership Team

BioGen Industries is guided by a multidisciplinary team of scientific and strategic leaders dedicated to advancing haemological research, developing innovative diagnostics, and ensuring responsible practice across our organisation.

Dr Octavian Juul

Managing Director

Dr Juul began his scientific career in the early 1970s, completing a doctorate in neurochemistry at the Université Descartes in Paris. His early research focused on metabolic deficiencies and enzyme-linked blood disorders, helping lay groundwork for several modern therapeutic approaches.

After senior appointments at the Wellside Foundation, Dr Juul co-founded BioGen Industries with Dr Katerina Marin. Under his leadership, the organisation has expanded its partnerships, supported early-career researchers, and established a stable research ecosystem for long-term development.

Though no longer a laboratory researcher, Dr Juul continues to oversee governance, strategy, and ethical direction across BioGen’s scientific portfolio.

Dr Katerina Marin

Director of Research

Dr Marin began her medical career as an endocrinologist before completing her doctorate focusing on clotting disorders, particularly Plasminogen Activator Inhibitor Type 1 deficiency. Her research experience spans institutions across Europe and the United States.

At BioGen, she leads the organisation’s research portfolio, overseeing programmes in rare disease investigation, emerging diagnostics, behavioural interactions, and long-term physiological outcomes associated with bloodborne conditions.

Dr Marin continues to direct several core projects and maintains a central role in the company’s scientific direction and international partnerships.

Johannes Rehema

Head of People

Mr Rehema brings extensive experience in organisational strategy, internal development, and corporate governance. He holds an MBA from the University of Cambridge Judge Business School, graduating with distinction.

Before joining BioGen, he worked at PricewaterhouseCoopers and Deloitte, specialising in restructuring and operational transformation. His work at BioGen focuses on staff wellbeing, regulatory compliance, and sustainable organisational growth.

Mr Rehema oversees HR policy, recruitment frameworks, internal culture, and long-term operational planning.
