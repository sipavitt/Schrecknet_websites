Who We Are

BioGen Industries is an independent research organisation located in North West London. Our mission is to support the advancement of medical knowledge through responsible scientific practice and meaningful collaboration across the healthcare sector.

What We Do

We investigate the mechanisms behind bloodborne illnesses, clotting disorders, and immune system interactions. Our teams combine laboratory research with clinical partnerships to explore new diagnostics, therapeutic pathways, and emerging technologies in haemology.

How We Support You

We offer paid participation opportunities for volunteer contributors, collaborative pathways for early-career researchers, and grant support for promising PhD candidates seeking to advance discoveries in the field of blood science.

Why Blood Research Matters

Blood plays a critical role in nearly every function of the human body. With more than 300 recognised blood disorders worldwide and millions affected by conditions ranging from anaemia to rare clotting diseases, advancing haemological research is essential to improving both diagnostic outcomes and long-term quality of life.
