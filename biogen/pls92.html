Project PLS-92: Platelet Stability Analysis

Lead Researcher: Dr A. Chen

PLS-92 examines platelet degradation rates in stored whole blood under varying temperature and anticoagulant conditions. The study aims to improve storage standards and extend shelf life for transfusion products.

Early findings suggest that citrate-based preservation methods show increased stability compared with EDTA equivalents. A comparative trial with ACD-A anticoagulant begins in Q3.
