Amir al Malik

Performer. Provocateur. Proprietor.

Amir founded The Second Coming as a response to the idea that pleasure should be hidden, apologised for, or sanitised.

This is a shop, yes — but also a club, a cabaret space, and a sanctuary for those who refuse to pretend they are something they are not.

Consent is our liturgy. Curiosity is encouraged. Hypocrisy is not.
